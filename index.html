Contacts + social platforms + identity management

Ever wanted to see if that one friend is online on one of the multiple platforms you use, with any of their multiple accounts? Don't want to open multiple VR games or check multiple apps like VRCX and NeosPeeps?

Onlivfe will be solution to that, and much more. It will show your social (VR) platforms' friends quickly in one place, without needing to open each platform individually.

Features

These are all just planned, the whole app is still WIP

  • Display online friends
  • Display worlds/sessions friends are in
  • Support for multiple platforms: VRChat, ChilloutVR, and NeosVR
  • Identity management
    • Multiple platform accounts per profile/peep
    • Share platform account to profile mappings
    • Easily add accounts from imported platform account mapping
